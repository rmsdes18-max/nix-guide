How It Works

What's coming with Nix:

  • New Archboss weapons — they seem very powerful.
  • New field boss items.
  • New field items — these kind of look like they'll be open-world items, like the open-world dungeon burning, maybe crack items. This might be a new open-world event, kind of similar to the Abyss event that came with the Abyss weapons.
  • Gear items — Hexapod considers these like mini-passives on weapons. There are two different types of gear items in the file: normal gear items and enchantments.
    • Gear items (mini-sockets): The way it was described perfectly, you might be able to socket items into your weapon. Maybe a weapon can have two or three sockets, because some of these gear items can benefit each other — if you have gear item one, you do this, and then another gear item boosts the effect of gear item one. This is why he still considers it like two or three sockets on our weapons.
    • Enchantments: Just kind of small little quality-of-life improvements — like more damage or slightly more cooldown.
  • Skills and passives: New skills and passives will be added in a new patch. Probably not additional skills but rather replacing existing skills, so we might be able to replace some of the skills we have.
